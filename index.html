We are ngOm.

In 2016 a team of three people started a small project for street kids in Karachi. The project has since then grown into an organization with 30 employees, running three major projects in the country, helping hundreds of women and children. We focus on supporting women and children in need. We believe that a country won’t flourish until women have equal rights and equal opportunities. We fight for women’s rights by providing education, access to healthcare and access to birth control. We believe that children are the future. We provide orphans and children from low-income families access to education, a save home and a community that cares.
