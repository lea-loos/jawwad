Karachi Women’s Shelter

In Pakistan’s biggest city, Karachi, we give shelter to women and their children in need. We support them in getting an education or training so they will be able to find a way to generate income and thereby gain independence longterm. We work with lawyers specialized in women's rigths issues that can help in cases of abuse, divorce and child custody cases.
