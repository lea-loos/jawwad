Lahore Rose Factory

In 2019 we set up a rose oil factory in collaboration with a rose farm close to Lahore. Both the farm and the factory are run by women entirely. Decisions are made by self-organizing teams. Besides essential rose oil we produce rosewater, rose soap and rose balm. Our products are sold locally and internationally. If you are interested in adding our products to your product line leave us a message.
