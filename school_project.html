Lahore Street School:

In 2016 a team of three Pakistanis started a small junior school with a class of eight kids in the suburbs of Lahore. By now the project has grown into a school with four classes of 20-25 children run by a staff of 12 teachers. This summer our first 4th graders will graduate. We are constantly looking for teachers due to the growing demand for places in our school. We are also planing to set up a middle school to give our pupils the chance to get a solid and free education.
